# Design Direction

## Aesthetic
- Vintage terminal / MS-DOS vibe
- Monospace fonts throughout
- Black and white base palette
- Hot pink / red crabs as the ONLY color accent — they pop against the monochrome
- Minimal, retro, clean

## Name Candidate
"Crab Fight" — short, punchy, memorable. Still considering.

## What Manuel Liked
- The dark background with colored accents in the CLI output
- The crab ASCII art
- The overall dark terminal feel of the prototype
- The contrast between monochrome UI and colorful interactive submissions in iframes

## Palette (refined)
- Background: #0a0a0a (near black)
- Text: #ffffff (white) + #888888 (gray for secondary)
- Borders/lines: #2a2a2a (dark gray)
- Cards: #111111 or #141414
- Crab accent: #FF2D6B (hot pink) or #FF3366 (red-pink) — the ONLY color
- Everything else is black, white, gray
- The colorful stuff lives inside the iframes (the submissions themselves)
